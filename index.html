The Problem: Manual Quote Processing

Meet Gavin at Alliance AMS

Gavin runs an automotive and industrial supplier in Perth. His customers (mine workshops, earthmoving companies) email him lists of part numbers. Then Gavin spends HOURS manually processing each quote.

Here's What Gavin's Manual Process Looks Like:

Gavin's Peach Software Screen:

Gavin opens his Peach inventory system and manually searches each part number one at a time. For every single part, he has to:

  • Search the part in Peach
  • Open supplier website in another window
  • Search for current price on their site
  • Copy the price
  • Paste it back into Peach
  • Repeat for next part...

This copying and pasting happens 10-30 times per quote, depending on how many parts the customer needs.

Toyota Dealer Email Quote:

For Toyota parts, Gavin can't even check prices himself online. He has to:

  • Email the Toyota dealer with his list of Toyota part numbers
  • Wait for THEIR staff to manually look up each part
  • Receive email back with pricing (sometimes hours later)
  • Manually enter all those Toyota prices into Peach

This adds another layer of waiting and manual work for every quote with Toyota parts.

Example Quote Complexity:

A typical quote for a mine workshop might include 20-30 parts from multiple categories:

  • Automotive parts: Brake pads, rotors, oil filters, belts
  • Toyota parts: Genuine parts requiring dealer pricing
  • Industrial supplies: Belts, bearings, seals
  • Safety equipment: Gloves, safety glasses, earmuffs
  • Tools and consumables: Paint markers, cable ties, tape

Each category comes from different suppliers. Gavin has to check them all individually, apply the right markup for this specific customer, then compile everything into one quote.
