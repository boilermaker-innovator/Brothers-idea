✦ What is iThoughts?
💡 The Concept
Every great idea started as one fleeting thought

iThoughts captures the thoughts you'd normally lose — driving home, mid-run, half-asleep. Drop them as pills on a card. When you've laid enough bricks, hit Refresh and AI synthesises them into new cards. The deck builds itself. You stay the author, always.
